Description and importance of survey results

The survey subjects are all employees who have worked in the company for a long time and have a basic understanding of the company. They believe that increasing sales, establishing brand awareness, developing new products and

expanding product range are the current goals of the company. They also hope that the company will become better and better by enhancing and improving the website and implementing online sales platform.

The correct investigation results will help the company determine the direction of the follow-up work
